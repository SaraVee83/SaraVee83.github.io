The EVA System

The Enhanced Visual Assessment solution for smart AI-powered colposcopy

What is this all about?

The EVA System is an FDA-cleared colposcope and enhanced visual assessment solution combining AI technology for cervical cancer screening with high-quality medical-grade image and video capture with secure online data management and services.

With our AI capabilities, cervical cancer screening is now available directly at the point-of-care, with accuracy higher and no wait compared to laboratory results.

Additional features:

  • Document patients - add annotations and filters directly to images for further review
  • Embedded telemedicine allows for remote consultation in real-time
  • Reduce unnecessary paperwork with EMR integration
  • Secure, encrypted, HIPAA and GDPR compliant online portal and data management services
